The Best VRML Resources On The Web

Keeping up with VRML is a full-time job, and there are a couple organizations who have made it their job to keep you up to date.

  • The VRML Repository -
    The San Diego Supercomputer Center has long been a driving force on the Web. This, IMHO, is the ultimate source for any VRML related information, be it the most recent browsers/tools, or links to some of the best worlds on the Web.
  • Inquiry.Com -
    Not only VRML, but a sort of repository of articles written about programming technology and issues.
